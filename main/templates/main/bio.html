Duo Veles is a musical collaboration between soprano {{ mateja.name }} and guitarist {{ pavel.name }}. The duo's musical journey began in 2015 when they met at a chamber music course in Trieste, Italy, held by Fedra Florit at the Conservatorio "Giuseppe Tartini." During their studies, Mateja and Pavel performed together at numerous concerts and cultural events.

After completing their studies, the duo's passion for music led them to continue the concert activity and musical research. Pavel and Mateja devoted themselves to developing their unique style and artistic personality, exploring a vast repertoire that includes music from various parts of the world, such as Italy, Spain, France, England, Brazil, Greece, and Belarus.

Duo Veles honed their skills at the prestigious Accademia Musicale Chigiana in Siena, under the guidance of Maestro Oscar Ghiglia in 2019. They received many accolades for their performances and were awarded a scholarship for their impressive accomplishments.

Veles collaborates with various festivals and concert producers, showcasing their passion and creativity through their captivating performances. Their partnerships include ACM Chamber Music Trieste, Piccolo Opera Festival, KarsiArt, Fondazione Vignola, Musica e Arte Cento, and Ikarus Festival, among others.
